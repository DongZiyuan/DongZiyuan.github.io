In summer 2020, I joined Alibaba Group as an SDE intern. I worked in a large team with other excellent engineers. Under the guidance of the mentor, I learned agile development, CI/CD pipeline, and how to write high-quality code.

Besides my regular work, I keep passionate about learning new technical skills. I often read technical blogs about new features of various development tools. I am also glad to share experience in open-source communities, communicate with senior engineers. Coding doesn’t occupy my life completely. Work-life balance is important to me. I enjoy traveling, doing sports, cooking, and watching movies, which could make me relax and be devoted to future work.
